Audit & Accounting

Comprehensive financial solutions combining independent audit verification with expert accounting services to ensure accuracy, compliance, and transparency.

Audit

Our Audit clients cut across all sectors of the Ghanaian economy and include regional and local owner-managed companies. Our audit approach is risk based and our methodology enables us to comply with requirements of international standards on auditing, and statutory and other professional requirements. Our approach is centered on:

  • Obtaining an understanding of the business entity and its operating environment.
  • Identifying and assessing the risks of material misstatement in the financial reports.
  • Designing strategies to respond to the risks.
  • Choosing appropriate procedures to respond to the risks and obtain sufficient, relevant evidence supporting significant financial statement assertions.
  • Carrying out rigorous quality control and review procedures over audit performance.
  • Forming an opinion and reporting under the applicable laws and standards.
  • Our procedures set standards on such matters as independence, confidentiality, training and quality control.

Accounting

Accounting services we provide include:

i. Bookkeeping

We prepare books of account for clients from basic records such as invoices, receipts, bills, payment vouchers and other relevant documents supporting transactions.

ii. Financial Statement Preparation

We prepare periodic financial statements and supporting details in accordance with statutory requirements and the International Financial Reporting Standards for audit by client auditors.

TAX

i. Provision of VAT Services

We assist clients to prepare monthly VAT returns and submit these to the Ghana Revenue Authority on their behalf.

ii. Payroll Services

Payroll outsourcing is a major service line for our Firm. We prepare payrolls for clients and arrange to pay statutory deductions to relevant authorities, including the Ghana Revenue Authority for taxes deducted and the Social Security & National Insurance Trust (SSNIT) and other pensions Fund Managers for pension deductions and contributions.

iii. Accounting Reconciliations

We assist clients to undertake complex reconciliation of accounts and balances.
