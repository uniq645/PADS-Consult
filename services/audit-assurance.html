Audit & Assurance

Independent examination of financial statements to ensure accuracy, compliance, and transparency for all stakeholders.

Overview

Our audit and assurance services provide independent verification of your financial statements, giving stakeholders confidence in the accuracy and reliability of your financial reporting. We conduct our audits in accordance with International Standards on Auditing (ISAs) and local regulatory requirements.

With our direct executive participation approach, you receive the expertise and attention of experienced professionals throughout the audit process, ensuring thorough examination and valuable insights into your financial operations.

Our Services Include

Statutory Audits

Annual audits required by law for companies, ensuring compliance with the Companies Act and accounting standards.

Compliance Audits

Verification of adherence to regulatory requirements, internal policies, and contractual obligations.

Feasibility Studies

Analysis of proposed projects or investments to determine viability and potential returns.

Financial Investigations

Detailed examination of financial records to uncover fraud, misappropriation, or irregularities.
