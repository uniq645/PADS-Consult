Tax Advisory Services

We provide a vast range of services to meet the domestic tax needs of our clients and we have, on various occasions, provided tax advisory services to various clients on their domestic operations and their international partners or related parties.

We provide innovative and creative ideas, as well as fulfil the compliance needs of clients.

Our Services Include:

  • Assistance in preparing tax documentation and reports meeting statutory requirements for submission to the Ghana Revenue Authority.
  • Support clients in preparing taxable income and declarations regarding annual results.
  • Provision of advice relating to corporate tax implication of business transactions referred to us.
  • Assistance to clients in dealing with assessment, correspondence and queries raised on corporate tax returns and advise management on any action required.
  • Update clients regarding new tax requirements in the country.
  • Provision of shareholder and owner-manager tax planning activities.
  • Apply for extensions, if applicable, in time to file annual returns.
  • Tax health checks to ensure clients regularly and continuously comply with requirements of the tax laws.
