About Business

Our Credentials

We are a dynamic knowledge driven public accounting firm serving local and international clients. We are committed to knowledge and best practice and we ensure efficient and effective services delivery to all our clients with client success central to our business approach. At PAD Consult Services we fulfil clients’ needs from concept to completion.

We serve growth-oriented owner-managed businesses with expanding local and international requirements, government agencies and companies, institution and non-profit organizations that require high quality specialist services at affordable costs. We have demonstrated, on jobs that we have executed, that rendering high quality services at affordable costs are achievable.

We ensure direct executive participation in all our engagement and we deliver flexibility, specialist expertise, closeness and personal service of a higher than average local firm standard in order that the needs of clients can be addressed timely. This is an advantage we possess over our competitors whose relationship with their clients may often prove to be impersonal or not time conscious.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the services provider of choice for organization that require high quality services at affordable costs.

We focus on how to add value to our client’s businesses by bringing together, to each assignment, a great array of expertise from our skilled professionals. We are dedicated to the provision of quality and timely services to meet client satisfaction.

Commitment
